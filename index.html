The Digital Dawn

Pixels glow like morning dew,
Binary stars in skies of blue.
A world of code, a soul of fire,
Climbing higher, ever higher.

Echoes of Spring

The garden wakes with a gentle sigh,
Underneath the pastel sky.
Petals dance on winds of gold,
Stories of the earth, retold.

Midnight Coffee

Warm ceramic, bitter bean,
The quietest hour I’ve ever seen.
Thoughts collide in the steam of night,
Waiting for the morning light.
